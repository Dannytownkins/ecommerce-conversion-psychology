# Post-Application Verification Checklist

After applying conversion guidance, verify every recommendation against this checklist. Any failed item must be addressed before the recommendation is final.

- [ ] Primary CTA is visually dominant and above the fold
- [ ] Trust signals are within proximity of conversion points (not footer)
- [ ] No more than 3 choices requiring simultaneous decision
- [ ] Page passes ethics gate (no dark patterns)
- [ ] Color contrast meets WCAG AA minimum (4.5:1 text, 3:1 UI components)
- [ ] Touch targets meet minimum 44x44px on mobile
- [ ] Price presentation is transparent — all fees visible before payment
- [ ] Social proof is specific and authentic (not vague or fabricated)
- [ ] Search and filters are visible, functional, and recover gracefully from zero-result states (category/collection pages)
