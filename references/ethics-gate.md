# Ethics Gate — Check FIRST, Override ALL

These rules are non-negotiable. They override every other recommendation in this plugin. Based on EU DSA Art 25, FTC Fake Reviews Rule (Oct 2024), CA SB-478 (Honest Pricing), CA SB-1001 (Bot Disclosure), and Epic Games $245M penalty precedent (superseded by Amazon Prime $2.5B settlement, Sep 2025).

## Urgency / Scarcity

- Countdown timers MUST be tied to real, verifiable deadlines — else BLOCK
- Scarcity claims ("Only X left") MUST reflect actual current inventory — else BLOCK
- "People viewing" counters MUST reflect real concurrent sessions — else BLOCK
- Fake urgency backfires: 17.6% sales DECREASE observed from fabricated "only X left" messages

## Pricing

- First displayed price MUST include all mandatory non-government fees — else BLOCK (CA SB-478)
- Strikethrough "was" prices MUST be real previous selling prices — else BLOCK
- Shipping costs MUST be disclosed before payment info collection — else BLOCK
- 39-48% of cart abandonment is caused by hidden costs revealed at checkout

## Reviews / Social Proof

- All reviews MUST be from real users of the product — else BLOCK (FTC Fake Reviews Rule)
- Insider/affiliate reviews MUST be clearly labeled — else BLOCK
- Negative reviews MUST NOT be suppressed or de-ranked — else BLOCK
- Penalty: up to $53,088 (2025 adjusted amount, effective January 17, 2025; FTC Federal Register) per violation (FTC)

## Choice Architecture

- Accept/decline options MUST be visually symmetrical — else BLOCK (EU DSA Art 25)
- No confirm-shaming language ("No thanks, I don't want to save money") — BLOCK
- No pre-checked boxes adding items or services — BLOCK
- Penalty: up to 6% global annual turnover (EU DSA)

## Subscription Dark Patterns

- Amazon Prime FTC settlement (Sep 2025): $2.5B ($1B civil penalty + $1.5B consumer refunds) — largest dark patterns enforcement action in history
- 35 million consumers affected by deceptive enrollment flows
- Settlement requires: clear decline buttons, transparent pricing disclosure, simple cancellation matching signup method
- Internal Amazon emails called practices "a bit of a shady world" — personal executive liability included
- Supersedes Epic Games ($245M) as the leading dark patterns precedent

## Override Rule

When in doubt, recommend the ethical alternative. Dark patterns are not just unethical — they are increasingly illegal and measurably counterproductive.

## Additional Regulatory Landscape (as of March 2026)

### EU AI Act (in force Feb 2, 2025)
- Prohibits AI systems deploying manipulative techniques to distort behavior
- Applies to AI-driven personalization, dynamic pricing, recommendation systems
- Penalties: up to 7% of global annual turnover
- Relevant to: personalization, AI-driven urgency signals, automated pricing

### GDPR
- Requires explicit consent for behavioral tracking and personalization
- Right to explanation for automated decision-making (Article 22)
- Penalties: up to 4% of global annual turnover or €20M

### CCPA/CPRA ADMT Regulations (effective Jan 1, 2026)
- Mandatory risk assessments for automated decision-making affecting consumers
- Applies to: personalized pricing, algorithmic recommendations, behavioral targeting
- Requires opt-out mechanisms for profiling

### US State Privacy Laws
- 20+ states now have comprehensive privacy laws active
- Indiana, Kentucky, Rhode Island: effective January 1, 2026
- Trend: convergence toward CCPA-like requirements with automated decision-making provisions

### CA SB 243 (2025)
- Supplements SB-1001 with stricter AI/chatbot disclosure requirements

### FTC Click-to-Cancel Rule — VACATED
- Eighth Circuit vacated the rule in July 2025; FTC restarting rulemaking
- ROSCA remains the primary federal subscription enforcement tool

### Enforcement Precedent
- EU DSA Art 25: X/Twitter fined €120M (December 2025), validating dark patterns prohibition
